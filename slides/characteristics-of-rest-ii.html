Characteristics and constraints of REST II

  • Uniform interface: All resources accessed through an interface based on following constraints:
    • Identification of resources
    • Manipulation of resources through their representations
    • Self-descriptive messages
    • Hypermedia as the engine of application state
  • Layered components: Intermediarys (Proxy servers, cache servers etc.) can be inserted to imporve scalabality and performance
  • Code-on-demand: Optionally, clients can be extended dynamically by servers providing executable code
